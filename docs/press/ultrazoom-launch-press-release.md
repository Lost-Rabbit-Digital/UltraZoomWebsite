# Ultra Zoom Brings Hover-to-Zoom to the Sites Free Extensions Can't Reach

A Chrome extension built for image-heavy professional workflows — real estate, insurance claims, e-commerce, design — works on MLSs, claim platforms, and marketplace dashboards where the popular free hover-zoom tools have failed for years.

**[CITY, State] – April 29, 2026** – Lost Rabbit Digital, an independent software studio, today launched Ultra Zoom, a Chrome extension that delivers hover-to-zoom on the websites where free alternatives have stopped working — including major real estate Multiple Listing Service (MLS) platforms, Zillow, Redfin, Realtor.com, insurance claim portals, and marketplace seller dashboards.

Image review is a daily, time-consuming task across many professions. Real estate agents check listing photos for defects before showings. Insurance adjusters review submitted claim photos for fraud signals. E-commerce buyers compare product images at full detail before sourcing. Designers and product researchers inspect assets across hundreds of sites a week. The free hover-zoom Chrome extensions that used to handle this work have been quietly broken on the highest-value sites for years; major MLSs in particular block the technique outright, leaving agents to open every photo in a separate tab to zoom in.

"Most real estate agents I've talked to have a workflow that involves opening every single photo in a listing in a separate tab, just to spot a hairline crack or a water stain," said David McHale, founder of Lost Rabbit Digital. "Ultra Zoom replaces that with a hover. The instant you put your cursor on a photo, the full-resolution version expands. The time savings compound across hundreds of listings a week."

Ultra Zoom installs from the Chrome Web Store and is verified working on Zillow, Redfin, Realtor.com, and major U.S. MLS systems including Bright MLS, CRMLS, Stellar MLS, MRED, and Northwest MLS. Beyond real estate, the extension targets verticals where image review is part of the daily job: insurance claim processing, e-commerce procurement, retail mystery-shopping, GIS analysis, and design-asset review.

The product is privacy-respecting. Image processing happens locally in the browser, no listing data leaves the user's device, and Lost Rabbit Digital does not collect or sell user data. A free trial is available on install; current pricing is published at https://ultrazoom.com. Lost Rabbit Digital is bootstrapped and is currently offering a 30 percent discount on the first year to real estate professionals using the code `REALTOR30` at checkout.
