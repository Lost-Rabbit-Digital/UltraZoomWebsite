Quick Guide & FAQ

How do I use Ultra Zoom?

After installing, hover over any image on a supported website. The full-size version will appear instantly. Press Esc to close, or scroll to adjust zoom level.

Which websites are supported?

Ultra Zoom works out of the box on 60+ sites including Google Images, Amazon, Reddit, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and more. You can also enable it globally for any website.

How do I disable it on a specific site?

Click the Ultra Zoom toolbar icon and use the per-site toggle to disable it for the current website while keeping it active elsewhere.

What are Pro features?

Pro includes video preview on hover, gallery navigation with arrow keys, one-click image downloading, and caption overlays.

Is my data collected?

No. Ultra Zoom does not collect or transmit any personal data or browsing history. See our Privacy Policy for details.
